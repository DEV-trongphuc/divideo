=== HUONG DAN KICH HOAT CARD DO HOA RTX 3050 CHO VOXCPM ===

De PyTorch nhan dang va chay tren card do hoa RTX 3050 (giup tang toc do sinh giong noi gap 30 lan, chi ton 2-5 giay moi slide), ban hay chay dung lenh sau trong terminal:

pip install torch torchaudio --index-url https://download.pytorch.org/whl/cu121 --force-reinstall

-----------------------------------------
Sau khi cai dat xong, kiem tra lai bang cach chay:
python diagnose.py

Ban se thay dong chu bao:
[OK] Successfully imported torch (va thiet bi se chuyen sang 'cuda')

Cuoi cung, chay lai he thong bang lenh:
node run_synthesis.js
